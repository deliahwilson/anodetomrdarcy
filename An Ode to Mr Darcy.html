An Ode to Mr. Darcy

From the 1995 BBC Adaptation of Pride and Prejudice

Have you seen the award winning BBC adaptation of Pride and Prejudice? Released in 1995, the six-episode series based on Jane Austen's novel of the same name is one of the greatest things you may ever see. Colin Firth plays Mr. Darcy and no matter how many other remakes have come since, he will always remain THE Mr. Darcy. In the image above you can see a still from the infamous wet shirt scene. If you haven't seen it, you simply must remedy that at your earliest convenience.
